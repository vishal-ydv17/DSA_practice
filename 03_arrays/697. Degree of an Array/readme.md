## Quick Overview
- **Problem Type**: Array, Hash Map  
- **Difficulty**: Easy  
- **Key Concept**: Track frequency and first & last positions  
- **Solution Summary**:  
  Use a hash map to record:
  - frequency of each number,
  - first and last occurrence index.  
  The degree is the max frequency, and the answer is the length of smallest subarray having that degree by checking the range of numbers with max frequency.
