## Quick Overview
- **Problem Type**: Dynamic Programming  
- **Difficulty**: Easy  
- **Key Concept**: DP Tabulation / Space Optimization  
- **Solution Summary**:  
  Compute the minimum cost to reach each step by taking the cheaper of:
  - cost to reach previous step,  
  - cost to reach two steps before.  
  The final answer is the minimum cost to reach the "top" (beyond last index).
