## Quick Overview
- **Problem Type**: Greedy, Heap  
- **Difficulty**: Easy  
- **Key Concept**: Max Heap / Priority Queue  
- **Solution Summary**:  
  Use a **max heap** to repeatedly smash the two largest stones:
  - Remove the two largest stones.
  - If they are unequal, insert the **difference** back into the heap.
  Continue until 0 or 1 stone remains; return the remaining weight or `0`.
